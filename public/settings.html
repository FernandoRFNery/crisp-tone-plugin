About Sentiment Scores

Sentiment scores range from -5 (very negative) to +5 (very positive), with 0 being neutral.

Example thresholds:
• -1.0: Moderately negative content
• -2.0: Significantly negative content
• -3.0: Very negative interactions only

Alerts trigger when messages contain profanity AND fall below your threshold.
